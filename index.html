I'm a photographer, videographer, and designer (and I guess programmer now, too!) who loves making visually cool things. Many of my artistic works tend to be about my life. I always try to use my power as an artist to tell the story of a person, place, or object. Much of the art I have experienced has influenced how I create things, such as the art seen at my hometown fair and music from inspirational musical talents like The Goo Goo Dolls, Taylor Swift, and Beyonce. In the case of photography, I take photos of things that I think are visually pleasing, that I find interesting, and of people who are important to me. I like all of my art to have a particular look that feels nostalgic, whether in its lighting, composition, or both.
